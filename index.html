xeokit - WebGL SDK for BIM & Engineering

xeokit is a software development kit from xeolabs for viewing 3D models in the browser. The SDK provides an extensible WebGL-based 3D engine with a bundled library of plugins focused on BIM and CAD. The engine uses advanced rendering techniques to view and interact with high-detail models in most major browsers, on both desktop and mobile platforms.
