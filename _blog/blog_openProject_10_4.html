xeokit in OpenProject 10.4

by xeolabs

Posted on January 1, 2020 PM

The xeokit SDK is in production within OpenProject 10.4!

Since October 2019, xeolabs has been working with OpenProject GmbH to develop the IFC model viewer within OpenProject 10.4.

The viewer is also available as a standalone BIM viewer, bundled as part of the xeokit SDK: https://github.com/xeokit/xeokit-viewer

To use the viewer, download it from GitHub, or fork the repository, then drop in your own models, and you're good to go.
