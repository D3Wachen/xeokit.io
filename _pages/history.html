The xeokit story unfolds epically

  • SceneJS

    xeolabs creates SceneJS, one of the first WebGL libraries.

    xeolabs develops the graphics engine within the BioDigital Human, using SceneJS.

  • SmileTrain Surgical Simulator

    xeolabs develops the SmileTrain Cleft Repair simulator, using BioDigital Human and SceneJS.

  • BIMSurfer

    xeolabs develops BIMSurfer v1, using SceneJS

  • SceneJS in OpenGL Insights

    xeolabs contributes a chapter on SceneJS to OpenGL Insights XXXX.

  • xeogl

    xeolabs develops xeogl, a WebGL library based on lessons learned from SceneJS.

  • xeokit

    xeolabs develops xeokit, a WebGL library based on lessons learned from xeogl, SceneJS, and their BIM applications.
